Problem Statement:-

LAN Gaming includes Call of duty Modern warfare 3 and FIFA 13.

Rules & Regulations:-

  • For COD MW3 each team consists of 6 members each.
  • For FIFA 13 1 player versus 1 player

FIFA:-

  • The total time for one game is 8 minutes(4+4).
  • Game will be one on one knock out round
  • The settings of the game will be standardized as default settings in case of any change in setting/s the participant will be disqualified.

CALL OF DUTY MODERN WARFARE 3:-

  • The game will be played in teams of 5 members
  • Game will be played in different pools on knockout basis
  • Time allotted for a match will be 10 minutes with unlimited score limit
  • The game will be played in Capture The Flag Mode

Judging Criteria:-

  • For COD MW3 matches will be as in league matches.
  • For FIFA 13 league or knockout according to the participation.

Team Size:-

  • FIFA:Game will be one on one knock out round
  • COD W3:The game will be played in teams of 6 members
