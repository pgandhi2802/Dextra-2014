I AM A PHYSICIST

A physicist is an atom's way of knowing about atoms

Introduction:-

A physicist is a scientist who does research in physics. Physicists study a wide range of physical phenomena in many branches of physics spanning all length scales: from sub-atomic particles of which all ordinary matter is made (particle physics) to the behavior of the material Universe as a whole (cosmology).

Problem Statement:-

In this event we'll find a participant who is expert in physics through an exciting quiz.

Rules & Regulations:-

  • This event will include two rounds, first round will contain written quiz with MCQ and one word answers and some audio visual rounds
  • Second level will include some fun with physics. We will mix physics with a well known game HOLLYWOOD BOLLYWOOD .In this level participant will be called on stage with its team.
  • We will make fill ups which they will have to fill by guessing alphabets, each wrong alphabet will reduce their probability of winning and each correct answer will increase their probability of winning.

Judging Criteria:-

In the First level we'll send only those teams to second round whose score would be more than the average score and in the Second round the highest points securer would be the winner.

Team Size:-

A team may contain a maximum of 3 members.

Eligibility Criteria:-

  • All students with a valid Registration Card of DEXTRA 2014 along with their respective educational institutes' ID Card are eligible to participate.
  • Non-JUET participants must possess an Accommodation Confirmation Letter (ACL) issued by the organizing body at the time of registration. Registration Card will not be issued without ACL.
