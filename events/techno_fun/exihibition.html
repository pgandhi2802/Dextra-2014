EXIHIBITION

The Technical Playground

Introduction:-

This is the last straw in the mega event DEXTRA-2014 .The exhibition will be the final contest for glory in DEXTRA-2014. The event will be a display of your love for the science and the scientific community. The exhibition will be an arena to display your innovative thinking and out of the box thoughts. The models made and created during DEXTRA will also be present in the exhibition if they display a devotion to the principles of technology.

Problem Statement:-

This is the ultimate playground so show us what you got!

The selection of a model for the exhibition from among the models created in the DEXTRA is totally upon the discretion of the organizing committee .But if you have a work of your own for display in the exhibition it will be admitted without any inspection from any member .The choice of the winners is totally upon the judging panel.

The fields where you can show your talent

  • Hardware
  • Art and event
  • Technical
  • Miscellaneous

Rules & Regulations:-

  • A serial number will be allotted to each team at the time of registration which needs to be displayed during judges' inspection.
  • Teams without serial number of exhibition will be disqualified immediately
  • Teams needs to inform event managers about requirements for their model at the time of registration.

Judging Criteria:-

It is totally depend upon the judging panel.

Team Size:-

A team can consist of maximum 2 participants.

Eligibility Criteria:-

  • All students with a valid Registration Card of DEXTRA 2014 along with their respective educational institutes' ID Cards are eligible to participate.
  • Non-JUET participants must possess an Accommodation Confirmation Letter (ACL) issued by the organizing body at the time of registration. Registration Card will not be issued without ACL
