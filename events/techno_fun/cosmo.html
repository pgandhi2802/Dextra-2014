COSMO

Shoot for the moon, You may fall on the stars

Introduction:-

Space is a mysterious place but there are plenty of things we are sure about when it comes to that vast space that surrounds us here on earth. Challenge what you think you know with our fun space quiz.

Problem Statement:-

Test your knowledge of planets, stars, moons, astronauts, our solar system, galaxy and more space related trivia.

Rules & Regulations:-

  • There will be three rounds. .
  • First round will be a quiz on general awareness about sky and astronomy.
  • Second will be an audio-visual round.
  • Third will be a buzzer round.

Judging Criteria:-

  • Highest scorer will be the winner.
  • In case of any discrepancy the decision taken by the jury will be treated as final decision.

Eligibility Criteria:-

  • All students with a valid Registration Card of DEXTRA 2014 along with their respective educational institutes' ID card are eligible to participate.
  • Non-JUET participants must possess an Accommodation Confirmation Letter (ACL) issued by the organizing body at the time of registration. Registration Card will not be issued without ACL
