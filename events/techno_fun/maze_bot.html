MAZE BOT

Mysterious Labyrinth

Introduction:-

The Crack the labyrinth is a kind of robot gaming in which robot can move in any possible direction. But, this is not the basic purpose of this robot. Robot can change its direction whenever any obstacle occurs in a particular direction. So the bot used in this is basically a helpful device so as to explore any particular hidden region which is beyond human intervention.

There are several techniques that can be used in solving mazes:

  • Random
  • Wall Following
  • Mapping

Problem Statement:-

Come and play the amazing maze given to u by organizer and participant have to show his / her talent to solve the maze in the most efficient way .Rules of the event will be declared on the spot.

Judging Criteria:-

  • The participant who will cross the maze in minimum time will be decided as the winner.
  • The contestant having the minimum score will be the winner.
  • Each second is equivalent to one point. E.g. If a contestant completes the maze in 70 sec. then its total score would be 70.
  • If the maximum time exceeds, then judging will be done on three basis.
    1. No. of checkpoints reached.
    2. No. of hits on the wall of maze.
    3. No. of blocks covered
  • In case of tie, the judging will be done on three basis.
  • No. of time bot hit the maze wall.
  • No. of blocks covered.
