TECH HUNT

Technical Fun Race Event

Problem Statement:-

This event will test your physical and mental capabilities as you will go through various fun filled hurdles and mind-boggling puzzles. In the end it’s about cracking the clues to get to the ultimate treasure.

Rules & Regulations:-

The event will take place in three rounds

Round 1: General screening test

All the participants will appear in this screening test which shall be based on simple aptitude and general awareness and technical questions.

Round 2:-Hurdle race (DAY 1)

80 members(max.) qualify for the second round divided into teams of 5 i.e 16 teams

1) Three legged race:-
  • After the completion of the race the team members will have to solve a crossword puzzle.
  • Disqualification: The rope should never open throughout the race.
2) Bull’s Eye:-
  • Shoot objects (Cups, balloons, balls etc) with mouser (pistol).
  • Answer to trivia related to the college.
3) Component fun:-
  • Team members will have to identify certain electronic components from a bunch of components kept in a bowl.
  • Disqualification: If more than 50% of the components are wrongly identified, no points will be awarded for this segment.
  • Solve a puzzle.
4) Tech charades:-
  • Disqualification: Any lip movement or voicing out the answer will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • All the 16 teams will be timed. Top 4 teams go through to the final round of Treasure hunt

Round 3 (DAY 2):- Treasure Hunt

  • All the 4 teams will simultaneously participate in this round.
  • Clues to the final treasure will be scattered throughout the college.
  • The teams will have to take hints from the clues for reaching one final place.
  • The team that reaches the first will win.

Team Size:-

  • Individual Participation in I round
  • I round qualifiers will participate in the second round in a team of 5. Teams will be formed on random basis.

Eligibility Criteria:-

All students with a valid Registration Card of DEXTRA 2014 along with their respective educational institutes' ID Card are eligible to participate.
