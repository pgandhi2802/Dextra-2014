KRIEGER'S RUMBLE

Face Your Within

Introduction:-

Different perspectives, different thoughts, different potentials, which ones you have?? Smart, intelligent, hard worker, if you think you are a complete package of all these qualities then have the guts to face the event and we’ll prove you wrong, this is the event to face yourself, rule out any possibilities of mercy, lifesavers, just you and your wits can lead you through all bones crushing, mind blowing, deceiving tasks. So buckle up yourself for a roller coaster ride to a place called hell and see the person within you shattering in front of your eyes.

Selection Procedure:-

Selection procedure is divided into three stages:

Round I:-IQ test

  • Verbal Reasoning.
  • Aptitude & Puzzle test.
  • General Awareness.

Note: Contestants crossing the cutoff mark will be qualified for next round.

Round II: Group Discussion

  • Topics will be General based on current issues.
  • Selected contestants will be eligible for next round.

Round III: Personal Interview

12 Contestants crossing all three rounds will be called as a Krieger, and they will be the part of Krieger’s journey.

Note: Journey comprises of different intelligent and daring tasks. Some tasks will be held in university campus and some will outside the university.

Rules & Regulations:-

  • Individual participation is required.
  • Once you get selected for the krieger’s journey, you cannot back out from the event.
  • If you are found indulging in unfair means, it may be manhandling your teammates, mentally challenging them, you would be bound to leave the event.
  • Disrespecting judges, use of abusive language is strictly prohibited.
  • In case of discrepancies all decisions taken by the juries would be final and unchallenged.

Judging Criteria:-

Round I:-IQ test

This will test all the participants on mental level, a challenging test to find the smartest one.

Round II: Group Discussion

This will test participant’s confidence, have guts to put your thought and stand by it till the end.

Round III: Personal Interview

It will select the most eligible contestant on basis of their overall quality. For journey your performance, social behavior and luck will lead to the final destination of rumble.

Team Size:-

There will be no teams formed by the participants, selection for the event would be done on individual basis, thereafter a total of 12 participants will be finally selected.

Eligibility Criteria:-

  • All students with a valid Registration Card of DEXTRA 2014 along with their respective educational institutes' ID card are eligible to participate.
  • Non-JUET participants must possess an Accommodation Confirmation Letter (ACL) issued by the organizing body at the time of registration. Registration Card will not be issued without ACL
