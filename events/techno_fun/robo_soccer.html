ROBO SOCCER

Let The Game Begin

Introduction:-

A soccer robot is a specialized autonomous robot and mobile robot that is used to play variants of soccer. Playing soccer with robots is an interesting challenge let’s accept and defeat the opponents.

Problem Statement:-

RoboSoccer is real time Soccer event in which teams have to come and play with the robots provided by Robotics Club. It’s not just about the robots, it’s a skill to master them and control them to win the castle.

Rules & Regulations:-

  • The teams will have to play the game with the robots provided by ISF.
  • Teams have to score maximum number of goals to proceed into the next round
  • The game will proceed according to standard rules of soccer, any changes will be discussed on the spot.
  • The team using unfair means during the event will be disqualified immediately

Judging Criteria:-

  • Round-1:-This will be the knock-out round and top 16 teams scoring maximum goals will qualify for round-2.
  • Round-2:-Round-2 will be the knock-out round between qualified 16 teams and top 8 teams will qualify for round-3.
  • Round-3:-This will be quarterfinal round and top 4 winners will reach into the semi-finals.
  • Round-4:-The semi-final round will give us our two great teams who have full control over their robots and who show a great skill of co-ordination.
  • Round-5:-This will be the final showdown between top 2 teams and winner will be decided based on the maximum number of goals scored.

Note:Team scoring maximum number of goals within the given span of 15 min will be the winner. (Extra Time will be given in case of tie).

Team Size:-

A team must consist of 4 members.

Eligibility Criteria:-

  • All students with a valid Registration Card of DEXTRA 2014 along with their respective educational institutes' ID card are eligible to participate.
  • Non-JUET participants must possess an Accommodation Confirmation Letter (ACL) issued by the organizing body at the time of registration. Registration Card will not be issued without ACL
