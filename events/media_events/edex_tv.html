Be the Face of DEXTRA

Introduction:-

A chance to become face of Dextra2014. This event requires a team of dedicated students to cover the whole techfest and make a professional video and present it as news broadcast.

Problem Statement:-

There will be no problem as student, they just need their camera and their experience with camera to make an effective video. They can use windows movie maker to make their final video.

Requiremnet For The Event:-

  • Identity cards for each member of each team
  • Cameras (teams will bring their own)
  • Mike (teams can use anything to make their mikes)

Judging Criteria:-

The final video will be judged on the basis of following points:

  • Attire
  • Communication Skills
  • Content of each and every event
  • Submission of Raw data
  • Graphical Representation of Final video
  • In case of any discrepancy the decision taken by the jury will be treated as final decision.

Team Size:-

Maximum team size is 3.

Eligibility Criteria:-

All students with a valid Registration Card of DEXTRA 2014 along with their respective educational institutes' ID Card are eligible to participate.
