OPEN SHUTTER

There are no rules for good photographs, there are only good photographs

Problem Statement:-

An online photography event consisting of three themes:-

  • Moment
  • Street
  • Nature

Rules & Regulations:-

  • One participant can send only one picture from each category.
  • Name, college name, contact details, email id, must be sent with photograph.
  • The photograph should be solely copyrighted to the participant.
  • No watermark/logo/border should be present in the photograph.
  • Basic post processing is permitted.
  • Picture containing smoking & drinking is strictly prohibited..
  • Photographs with good numbers of facebook likes will be shortlisted and best among that will be declared as winner.
  • The students/staff of JUET or the judges or the sponsors are not responsible for the copyrights of the photograph that is uploaded.
  • Entries received after April 13 will not be considered.

Judging Criteria:-

  • Photographs with good numbers of facebook likes will be shortlisted and best among that will be declared as winner.
  • 1 participant from each category will be selected as winner.

Team Size:-

Stand alone event.
