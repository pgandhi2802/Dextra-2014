AutoSPARK

(Workshop on Automobile Mechanics & IC Engine Design)

Content of the workshop:-

Day 1:-

  • Session-1:- Introduction (1 Hr)

    What is an automobile ?,Brief history,Changes over the years,Indian automobile industry,Sigma ratings

  • Session-2:- Chassis Design (1 Hr)

    Brief terminology,Types of Chassis

  • Session-3:- Suspension Unit (1 Hr)

    Brief terminology,Types of Suspension

  • Session-4:- Braking Unit (1 Hr)

    Disc Brakes,Drum brakes,Magnetic Brakes,Vaccum Brakes,Anti-lock Braking System,Brake Actuators,Power Brakes and Master Cylinders,Brake Fluids

    Query Session(30 min)

  • Session-5:-Designing Using Software-Basics of AutoCAD & CATIA V5 (2 Hr)

    Drawing,Modifying & Dimensions in AutoCAD,Sketching & Part Modelling in CATIA

    Query and Competition(1 Hr)

Day 2:-

  • Session-5:-Designing Using Software-Basics of AutoCAD & CATIA V5 (1 Hr Continue ...)
  • Session-6:-Transmission System(2 Hr)

    Types of Transmission System,Differentials,2WD,4WD,AWD,Tyres and Traction Control

  • Session-7:-IC Engines (2 Hr)

    • Types,Compression Ignition,Spark Ignition,Layout,Engine Balancing,Spark Plug,Carburetor,Fuel Injector,Valves & Valve Timing,Valve trains,Engine Cooling,Turbochargers,Superchargers,Air/Fuel Ratio,Wankel Engines(6 Stroke),Latest Technologies,PGMFi,DTS-Fi,MPFI,CRDI,RTR,WTi

  • Session-8:-Air Bags & Steering System (1 Hr)

    Live Engine Demonstration-HOnda 110 CC(45 Min) Query and Competition(1 Hr)

  • HANDS-ON:-

    Basics of designing software i.e. CATIA V5 or Solidworks

    Learning of whole Automobile unit live Engine and car (if possible) through demonstration.
